about ycreators

Ycreators is a prep school for continued learning after class. Our mission is to create life-changing experiences with STEAM eduacation that embolden students to shape the future. Our learning experience provides gamified hands-on experience. We help our creators push their boundaries by creating an engaging learning curriculum blending Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts & Crafts, and Mathematics to explore the creative universe of these young minds.

With our extraordinary, passionate and qualified instructors, our programs are built to make your child stand out of the crowd and be the best they were born to be.

our courses

Programming

Your child will learn to assemble and troubleshoot their hardware and then it's time to boot up the computer—that they will build—to experience the thrill of coding in Python. With an introduction to hardware, engineering, and coding, this course has something for everyone.

Ages: 8 - 15

Duration: 3 months

3D Modelling

Imagination meets manufacturing in this hands-on course where students learn how to design simple 3D models and prepare them for 3D printing. Learn how to change the filament, maintain a 3D printer, and operate it safely.

Ages: 6 - 15

Duration: 3 months

Artificial Intelligence

What if your code could turn pictures of horses into zebras, tell the difference between cats and dogs, and beat world champions at chess? Step aside sorcery, machine learning is here and performing tasks people used to believe we're only possible in fantasy worlds.

Ages: 10 - 15

Duration: 3 months

IOT & Smart Devices

IOT is changing the way we live. With more and more devices getting connected to the internet, the demand for skills in this area is very high. You can learn IOT only by building projects - get your hands on the sensors and actuators, set up the network and collect & analyse data sent by the sensors.

Ages: 8 - 15

Duration: 3 months

how ycreators work

Join our program

Apply today to one of our creator's courses. We focus on finding the highest-quality teachers. You can expect to receive your acceptance email within 3 working days. Our onboarding process helps your child ease into our program.

Learning with peers

Your child will join a group of 5 to 10 students with a dedicated teacher for weekend classes and weekly virtual check-in sessions. Our programs are designed to stimulate your child mentally and optimize his creative side.

Growth

Our program is designed to equip your child with skills that will stay with them for a lifetime. Empathy. Critical thinking. Resilience. Confidence. Collaboration. For them everything is possible.
